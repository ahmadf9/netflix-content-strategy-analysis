# 🎬 Netflix Content Strategy Analysis

## 📌 Project Summary
This project analyzes Netflix movie and TV show data to identify patterns in **content quality, popularity, genre performance, and yearly content trends**. The analysis aims to generate business insights that can support **content acquisition, production planning, and portfolio strategy**.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
Netflix operates with a large and diverse content catalog across multiple genres, countries, languages, and release years. However, not all content performs equally in terms of quality and popularity.

This project answers key business questions:
- Which content types and genres perform best?
- How do ratings and popularity change over time?
- Are highly rated contents always popular?
- What content strategy opportunities can be identified from the data?

Dataset source: [Kaggle - Netflix Movies & TV Shows till 2025](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/bhargavchirumamilla/netflix-movies-and-tv-shows-till-2025)

- Rating

- Country
- Language

---

## 🔍 Analysis Process
1. **Data Cleaning**
   - Checked missing values
   - Standardized columns and data formats
   - Combined movie and TV show datasets

2. **Exploratory Data Analysis**
   - Analyzed rating distribution
   - Compared popularity and rating trends
   - Evaluated genre-level performance
   - Examined yearly content trends

3. **Business Insight Generation**
   - Identified high-quality content segments
   - Compared popular vs highly rated content
   - Highlighted content strategy opportunities

---

## 📈 Key Findings
- Content ratings show a **gradual upward trend with several fluctuations**
- Popularity remained relatively stable until a sharp increase in **2024–2025**
- Some niche genres achieved **high average ratings despite lower content volume**
- High popularity does not always indicate high content quality
- Recent content appears to gain stronger audience attention compared to earlier years

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Prioritize investment in **high-rated niche genres** to strengthen content differentiation
- Use popularity spikes in 2024–2025 as a signal for future acquisition strategy
- Balance content decisions between **audience demand** and **content quality**
- Monitor genre performance regularly to identify emerging content opportunities
- Develop dashboard tracking for rating, popularity, genre, and release-year trends

---

## ⭐ STAR Method

### Situation
Netflix has a large content catalog with variations in genre, rating, popularity, country, language, and release year. A structured analysis is needed to understand content performance and identify strategic opportunities.

### Task
Analyze Netflix content performance based on rating, popularity, genre, content type, country, language, and yearly trends to support data-driven content strategy.

### Action
Performed data cleaning, merged movie and TV show datasets, conducted exploratory data analysis, created visualizations for rating and popularity trends, analyzed genre performance, and translated findings into business recommendations using Python.

### Result
The analysis found that Netflix content ratings generally improved over time with some fluctuations, while popularity increased sharply in 2024–2025. Several niche genres showed strong rating performance despite lower content volume. These insights can help support more effective content acquisition and production decisions.
